Treasurist Manifesto

by Pulp Bitcoin

If bitcoin is digital, why turn it into pulp?

Brain wallets are the gold standard for pricacy and security, and a printed magazine gives you a paper workbook to memorize your wallet. With Pulp Bitcoin you won't need to worry about keyloggers nor computer hackers.

Stories are memory and hold the key to treasuring bitcoin inside your mind.

Every Pulp Bitcoin magazine issue will use mnemonic games to memorize a passphrase and:

  • publish every ten kilo blocks, starting at block 630k
  • have 10 - 12 illustrated stories
  • each illustration will allow for easy memorization of 2 or 3 bitcoin passphrase words
  • look like a classic 7' x 10' pulp fiction magazine with over 100 pages

Our mnemonic illustrations will evoque the "negative space" around your story. For example: an unamed victim, an undisclosed location, or any character that could take a proper name.

To facilitate memorization we will look for oportunities to reanimate public domain mythos, from Herbert West, to Hermes Trimegisto, and otherwise any resemblance with reality is fictional coincidence.

Public submissions can be done via github. Original authors will be published together with their bitcoin address qr code. Pulp Bitcoin pays to the published author address with the equivalent to $200 at the time of print. The author may continue receiving bitcoin donations to their published addresses.
